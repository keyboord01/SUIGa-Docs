# About SUIGa

SUIGa was conceived during a SUI hackathon held in Istanbul, at ModaPalas.

## Mission

Our mission is to enhance the gaming experience on the SUI blockchain by providing a unified platform where gamers can discover, play, and engage with a wide variety of games. SUIGa aims to foster a vibrant community where achievements are celebrated, friendships are formed, and players have a significant role in shaping the future of gaming on the SUI network.

Thank you for visiting our About page! Explore our platform to see the innovative features and community-driven services that make SUIGa a unique and exciting place for all SUI blockchain gamers.
